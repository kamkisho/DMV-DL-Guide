Traffic Signals

Red Light

Come to a complete stop.

Right Arrow

Come to a complete stop.

Right turn on Red Light

Come to a complete stop. Look both ways and yield the right-of-way to pedestrians and other traffic.

Left turn on Red Light

No left turn on red light unless you are on a on-way street and you are turning left onto another one-way street.

Right turn on Red Light

Come to a complete stop. Look both ways and yield the right-of-way to pedestrians and other traffic.
